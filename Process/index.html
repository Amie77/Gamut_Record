Branding

What does the company do, or offer?

Gamut Record sells phonograph records of artists from all time. We offer a variety of music selections for music lovers to discover hidden gems of the music industry.


Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

The target audiences are music lovers from 20 to 35 years old who are interested in collecting phonograph record.


Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Most of the best phonograph record shops in Vancouver are in downtown area. For people who live far away or are too lazy to get there, can simply open their computer and shop on this website for a better price. Comparing to other stores' website, our graphics and branding will be more compelling in terms of color and graphic design. The company selects only timeless pieces or albums with compelling covers to sell.

Color Scheme


Red: #FF4534

This color is used in the hero image and remove button in the shopping cart section.

Pink: #FFCDD2

This color only appears when a link is hovered. Right now, it also appears in the imageholders at the products section.

Blue: #519AF6

The blue color is used for the background color of buttons. It's in the hero image as well.

Green: #04E78B

Since the green color can give off a fresh and positive sense, it is mainly used as the background color of buttons when clicked. It also appears in the hero image.

Yellow: #FFC95D

This color mainly appears as the background color of section titles, and is used in the hero image. It is also the color of text when a link is clicked/focused.
