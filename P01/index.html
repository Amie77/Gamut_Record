Interactive Elements

In-text link

This is an in-text link.

Sample code:

<p>This is an <a class="intext_link" href="#">in-text link</a>.</p>

Button Link

Default Button

Sample code:

<a href="#" class="button">Default Button</a>

Button Link

Default Button

Sample code:

<a href="#" class="button">Default Button</a>

Submit/Reset Buttons

Sample code:

<form action="/">
  <button type="submit" value="Submit">Submit</button>
  <button type="reset" value="Reset">Reset</button>
</form>

Text Input Field with Label

Sample code:

<form action="/">
  <label>Name:</label> <input type="text" placeholder="Jo Zhou">
</form>

Radio Button with Label

Sample code:

<form action="/">
  <label class="form_text">Gender :</label>
  <label class="form_radio"><input type="radio" name="sex"> Male </label>
  <label class="form_radio"><input type="radio" name="sex"> Female </label>
</form>

Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Our company is an online record/album company, called Gamut Record. We offer online order and deliver. We sold records and albums including CDs and vinyls.

Who is the company to potential customers?

Our main potential customers are music lovers, including fans of singers or idols and vinyl collectors.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

We offer a 30-second listening preview to make sure users can find their favorite music. At the same time we are an online purchase with home delivery Service. We can provide convenience to customers, so that they can order their favorite album anywhere at anytime.
