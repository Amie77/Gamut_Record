Basic Branding Ideas

About the Company

Gamut Record is an online vinyl record store that strives to provide niche records with good-looking cover design. The overall genre of the long-lasting records selling in the store would be jazz, alternative and downtempo music. Helping audiences to have a visual sense of music is one of the dominant values of Gamut Record in terms of attracting customers’ choices. As the target customers of Gamut Record is female-centred, visual appealing is an essential factor for females to consider when making shopping decision.

Potential Customers

The target customers for Gamut Record are young people (mainly female) aged from 20-35, interested in long-playing record collection espcailly those with good-looking cover design.

Features that attract customers

Visual appealing record is one of the biggest adventages makes Gamut Record stand out from those regular record stores.
